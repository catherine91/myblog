2018 trip to Sedona, AZ

In 2018 I visited Sedona, AZ for the very first time. I was amazed by the natural beauty. I hiked up to Chapel of the Holy Cross. I would recommend visiting in January/February as it will be less crowded and the weather will still be good.

-Catherine T

2019 trip to Las Vegas, NV

In Spring of 2019 I took a trip to Las Vegas to visit a friend. There is a lot to do in Vegas besides gambling and drinking! While visiting the casinos can be fun, I also enjoy going to the Red Rock mountains, shopping in downtown Summerlin, and going to Fremont Street.

-Catherine T

2020 trip to San Francisco, CA

On New Years Day of 2020 I drove into San Francisco and went to Fisherman's Wharf. I was nearby for work and thought what better way to spend the day than visit the city. From my first time visiting at age 20, I have always loved SF. It never disappoints!

-Catherine T
